Elegant Floral Jacquard Lamp Shades with Brass Fitters

Enhance your lighting setup with these beautifully crafted Empire lamp shades from Aspen Creative. Featuring a stunning floral jacquard fabric, these shades are designed to elevate any room with a touch of classic elegance and charm. The durable virgin styrene backing board keeps them intact and vibrant, ensuring long-term use without the risk of cracking or yellowing that often affects recycled materials.

Key Features

  • Floral jacquard textured fabric for elegant design
  • Brass clip-on fitter for secure attachment
  • Empire shape with 3" top, 6" bottom, and 5" slant height
  • Durable virgin styrene backing board for long-lasting use
  • Ideal for bare torpedo chandelier bulbs with E12 base

Why Choose These Lamp Shades?

Whether you're looking to refresh your existing lighting fixtures or add a decorative accent to your space, these lamp shades deliver style, functionality, and a warm, diffused glow that creates an inviting ambiance. Perfect for use with E12 base bulbs, they bring a timeless aesthetic to your home decor while maintaining a secure fit on bare torpedo chandeliers.

Perfect for Every Room

These lighting accessories are a versatile addition to any modern home, bringing a fresh artistic look to your lighting setup. Their unique design makes them an ideal focal point for those who appreciate a blend of sophistication and warmth in their interior spaces.
