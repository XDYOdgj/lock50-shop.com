Elegant Empire Shape Lamp Shades

Enhance your space with the refined charm of these timeless lamp shades. Designed to blend classic and modern styles, they bring a sophisticated touch to any room, from living rooms to bedrooms and beyond.

Timeless Design & Quality Craftsmanship

Featuring an authentic Empire shape, these lamp shades are a perfect match for those who appreciate elegant, transitional lighting. The use of Virgin Styrene backing board ensures durability, resisting cracking and yellowing over time for long-lasting beauty.

The off white color offers a soft yet warm lighting effect, creating a more inviting and cozy ambiance. Compared to traditional shades made with recycled materials, this design provides a refined, natural glow that enhances your decor effortlessly.

Easy Installation & Versatile Use

With a clip-on style, these shades are designed to attach directly to bare Torpedo bulbs, delivering secure and hassle-free installation. They are not recommended for bend tip bulbs and instead offer a more reliable fit for standard E12 candelabra base bulbs.

A great solution for updating your lighting setup or replacing old shades, this set of 2 is ideal for adding a refined, customizable look to your space. Whether you're looking to refresh your interior or personalize your lighting, these shades offer practical value without sacrificing style.

Key Features

  • Authentic Empire shape design for elegant lighting
  • Made with Virgin Styrene backing board for durability
  • Off white color with soft, natural lighting effect
  • Clip-on style fits bare Torpedo bulbs, bend tip not recommended
  • Set of 2 shades for versatile use in different settings
