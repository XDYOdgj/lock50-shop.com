Elevate Your Lighting with Aspen Creative 51020-2 Lamp Shades

These exquisite lamp shades are designed to bring a touch of refined beauty to your home decor. Handcrafted from premium gold linen textured fabric, they create a distinctive, luxurious look that enhances the warmth and charm of any room.

Featuring a sturdy brass clip-on fitter, these lamp shades offer effortless installation and secure attachment. The traditional Empire design, with its 3" top, 6" bottom, and 5" slant height, ensures a seamless fit for most light fixtures.

Built with virgin styrene backing board, these shades are not only durable but also provide an even light distribution, ensuring your space stays bright and inviting for years to come. Their ability to resist cracking and yellowing adds to their longevity and style retention.

Ideal for use with bare torpedo bulbs, these shades are especially suited for accent lighting in living rooms, dining areas, or entryways. Whether you're modernizing a chandelier or enhancing a wall sconce, they deliver unmatched versatility and elegance.

Perfect for both candelabra and medium base lamps, these lamp shades are a must-have for anyone looking to upgrade their lighting setup with a touch of sophistication and timeless design.
