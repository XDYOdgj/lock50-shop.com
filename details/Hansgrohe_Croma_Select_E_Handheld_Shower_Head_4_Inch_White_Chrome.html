Hansgrohe Croma Select E Handheld Shower Head

Elevate your daily shower routine with this stylish and efficient handheld shower head, combining modern design with advanced functionality.

Sleek & Elegant Design

Featuring a modern look with a sleek white and chrome finish, this shower head is designed to complement any bathroom decor. Its minimalist aesthetic adds a sense of luxury and refinement to your space.

Versatile 3 Jet Modes

Choose from three different jet types: SoftRain for a gentle and calming shower, IntenseRain for a powerful and invigorating spray, and Pulsating Massage for a spa-like experience. Whether you're unwinding or energizing, this head delivers the perfect setting.

Water Conservation

With a low water flow of 1.75 GPM, this eco-conscious shower head saves water without sacrificing comfort or performance. It’s a smart choice for environmentally aware households.

Easy Maintenance

The QuickClean system makes maintenance effortless. Simply wipe the silicone nozzles to remove limescale buildup, ensuring a hassle-free and long-lasting shower experience with every use.

Luxurious Performance

Experience pristine water flow with the XXL performance feature. This shower head ensures a steady, comfortable flow, making it ideal for those who value both quality and ease of use.

Perfect for Modern Living

Whether you're part of a young family or looking to update your bathroom, the Croma Select E offers a modern upgrade that blends style with practicality. Its intuitive control button lets you switch jet modes quickly and conveniently.

Simple Installation

Installs easily with a shower hose and arm, making it a great addition to your bathroom setup. For the best results and to ensure safety, Hansgrohe recommends professional installation.
